Kosta Ristić (Serbia, 1990) is a young Film and Theatre director. His short and feature films were premiered and awarded on major international film festivals (Cannes, Sarajevo, Cottbus, Belgrade, Buenos Aires, Kustendorf, Los Angeles, Prague). He is known for working with kids in film and theatre.

Coming from professor’s family, Kosta was always interested in wider education. In early days he was junior basketball player and awarded amateur kid actor. He was also awarded competitor in mathematics, so he enrolled and finished Mathematical Grammar School. He then graduated and mastered on Faculty of Dramatic Arts. In 2017 he attended masterclass of celebrated director Werner Herzog on Escuela International de Cine y TV. He is honorary titled Magister Humanitas by Faculty of Education. He is a member of representative Association of Film Artists of Serbia.
