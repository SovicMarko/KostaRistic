MODERN EDUCATION IN ACTION
Information age changed all the aspects of everyday life and work. The greatest challenge comes for education. It is not only the form, but also the content of education is changing. Not just writing on boards, but also learning informations that can be easily found on the internet lost its primary function. Thus the focus of education becomes artistic, scientific and technological creativity, social and organisational skillness. More holistic approach is arising, especially in children education. Old school motto was “learn, to create” and modern school motto is “create, to learn”.

Our approach to modern education is based on film. From the first to the fourth industrial revolution film stood out as a revolutionary juncture of art and science. It is “total art”, summarum of all the arts, mixed with technology and social organisation. It is expression of life in all of its aspects.

Through the process of making films, children can play on a higher level. It is a game that leads them into the world of thoughts and ideas. Learning kids to make films is our secondary goal, the primary one is to inspire them to think about thinking and find enthusiasm in creativity and joint work. It is modern education in action.
